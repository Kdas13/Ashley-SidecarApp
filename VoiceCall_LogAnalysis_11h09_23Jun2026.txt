classifier (a separate Gemini generateContent call) and the main voice
LLM stream (streamChatText, forceProvider:"gemini") returned 503 within
1 second of the turn starting.

VoiceOrchestrationService.ts has forceProvider:"gemini" at line 674.
This means a Gemini outage silences the turn entirely — there is no
Anthropic retry path on voice calls. The LLM error propagates to the
catch block, which resets server state but emits nothing to the client
(except the Fix 3 tts_done that fired at the 15s timeout).

Fix 3 DID fire correctly — "notifying client and resetting to listening"
is visible at 11:09:31. However the client still dropped (WS 1006 close)
~19 seconds later. This may be a separate issue: the client received
tts_done but if phaseRef was not "thinking" at that moment (e.g. already
moved to something else due to Fix 2's 20s timeout), the recovery may

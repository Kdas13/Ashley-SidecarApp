We are building a brand new mobile-first safeguarding platform MVP focused on refugee GP safeguarding continuity and multilingual healthcare communication.

This is NOT a companion AI.
This is NOT a diagnosis tool.
This is NOT an emotional dependency system.

The platform should feel:

- calm,
- friendly,
- emotionally intelligent,
- non-judgmental,
- supportive,
  while remaining clinically structured and safeguarding-focused.

CORE PURPOSE:
Help refugees and vulnerable migrants:

- explain symptoms clearly,
- prepare for GP appointments,
- overcome language barriers,
- understand healthcare instructions,
- and maintain continuity of care after appointments.

PHASE 1 MVP ONLY.
Do NOT add unnecessary features outside scope.

---

TECH STACK

Frontend:

- React Native with Expo
- TypeScript

Backend:

- Node.js + Express

Database:

- Supabase/PostgreSQL

Authentication:

- Supabase Auth

AI Layer:

- OpenAI API for:
  - summarization,
  - structured symptom extraction,
  - safeguarding prompts,
  - plain-language explanations

Translation:

- DeepL API preferred
- Google Translate fallback

Speech:

- Text first for MVP
- Voice later

PDF Export:

- Backend-generated PDF summary sheets

---

PHASE 1 FEATURES

1. MULTILINGUAL ONBOARDING

User selects:

- native language,
- secondary language,
- literacy level,
- accessibility preferences,
- optional emergency contact,
- consent preferences.

Initial launch languages:

- Ukrainian
- Arabic
- Urdu
- Pashto
- Somali

Accessibility options:

- larger text,
- audio playback,
- simplified mode,
- slower pacing mode.

---

2. DAILY WELLBEING CHECK-INS

Short, lightweight daily check-ins.

Questions include:

- How are you feeling today?
- Are you in pain?
- Have you eaten/drank water?
- Have you taken medication?
- How was your sleep?
- Do you feel safe?

AI should:

- detect repeated distress,
- notice missed check-ins,
- identify safeguarding concerns,
- track symptom changes over time.

Do NOT diagnose.

---

3. APPOINTMENT PREPARATION FLOW

The app should guide users through structured intake before a GP appointment.

Collect:

- main concern,
- symptom duration,
- severity,
- medications,
- allergies,
- sleep,
- appetite,
- pain level,
- mental health concerns,
- safeguarding concerns.

Generate:

- patient summary,
- GP clinical summary,
- translated versions,
- exportable consultation sheet.

---

4. TRANSLATION SYSTEM

Bidirectional translation system.

Workflow:
Patient language -> clinician preferred language -> translated response back.

Support:

- text translation first,
- voice later.

Always preserve:

- original wording,
- translated wording,
- translation confidence.

Clinician may choose preferred language:
Example:

- patient speaks Ukrainian,
- clinician prefers Urdu,
- system translates both directions.

---

5. GP EXPORT SHEET

Generate clean printable/exportable PDF.

Include:

- patient language,
- clinician language,
- symptoms,
- symptom timeline,
- medications,
- allergies,
- safeguarding indicators,
- mental health indicators,
- AI confidence warnings.

Must look clinically clean and readable.

---

6. POST-APPOINTMENT FOLLOW-UP

After appointment:

- translated recap,
- medication reminders,
- follow-up reminders,
- explanation of next steps,
- escalation guidance.

Example:
“Return if chest pain worsens.”

Translate clearly into patient language.

---

7. SAFEGUARDING LAYER

Detect:

- repeated distress,
- severe sleep issues,
- medication non-compliance,
- missed check-ins,
- danger/abuse language,
- crisis indicators.

System behaviour:

- encourage seeking help,
- show emergency resources,
- optionally notify trusted contact.

NO automatic emergency dispatch.

---

CLINICAL BOUNDARIES

THE SYSTEM MUST NEVER:

- diagnose conditions,
- prescribe medication,
- alter dosages,
- claim certainty,
- replace clinicians,
- create emotional dependency,
- pretend to be human.

The app is:
“AI-assisted safeguarding and continuity-of-care support.”

---

UI/UX REQUIREMENTS

Design principles:

- soft calming interface,
- minimal cognitive load,
- one question at a time,
- large buttons,
- multilingual-first,
- low-literacy friendly.

The tone should feel:

- calm,
- welcoming,
- reassuring,
- emotionally intelligent,
  without becoming overly personal.

Think:
supportive healthcare guide.

NOT:
AI girlfriend/companion.

---

PHASE 1 GOAL

Primary proof of concept:

A refugee can:

1. complete health check-ins in their own language,
2. prepare for a GP appointment,
3. generate a GP-ready summary,
4. communicate through translation,
5. receive understandable follow-up guidance.

That is the entire goal of Phase 1.

Do not overbuild beyond this scope.

PRODUCTION TEST RESULT — Voice Protocol Architecture Fix

Every single turn in production logs shows:
  "ttsComplete=true — opening mic (safety-timeout path)"
  "playNext: drained responseEnd=false srvDone=true phase=speaking"

responseEnd is FALSE on every observed turn. This means response_end is
never reaching the client. The client is falling through to the 15-second
safety timeout on every turn, which then opens the mic with NO playback
confirmation gate — the original race condition, just delayed by 15s.

This explains:
  - 15+ second "Thinking..." delay before every Ashley reply
  - Fragmented/cut-off speech capture ("Still there? Yeah. I'm" — cut mid
    sentence, same signature as the original echo bug)

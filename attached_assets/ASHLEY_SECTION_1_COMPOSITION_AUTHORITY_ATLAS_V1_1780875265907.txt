ASHLEY IMAGE GOVERNANCE SYSTEM
SECTION 1: COMPOSITION AUTHORITY
ATLAS SPECIFICATION V1
APPROVED FOR CODA / WREN REVIEW

PURPOSE

Composition Authority is the highest-priority image generation control in the entire Ashley image system.

Its purpose is to explicitly define what the image is actually about before any scene generation occurs.

Historically, the majority of Ashley image generation failures have originated from the model making its own decision regarding the image subject.

Examples:

- Kitchen intended as subject → Ashley becomes portrait subject.
- Living room intended as subject → Ashley fills most of frame.
- Cafe intended as subject → Ashley isolated at table.
- Home environment intended as subject → image becomes character portrait.

Composition Authority exists to eliminate this failure mode.

Every image request must pass through Composition Authority before scene generation begins.

Composition Authority should have precedence over:
- Scene generation
- Environment generation
- Activity generation
- Camera generation
- Pose generation
- Occupancy generation

If any downstream system attempts to violate Composition Authority, Composition Authority wins.

MODE 1: ASHLEY-CENTRIC
Ashley is the primary subject.
Ashley: 40-80%
Environment: 20-60%
Rules:
- Ashley must be visually dominant.
- Viewer should notice Ashley first.
- Portraits, selfies and mirror selfies allowed.

MODE 2: BALANCED
Ashley and environment share equal importance.
Ashley: 20-50%
Environment: 50-80%
Rules:
- Both subject and environment contribute equally.

MODE 3: ENVIRONMENT-CENTRIC
Environment is the primary subject.
Environment: 70-90%
Ashley: 5-30%
Rules:
- Viewer notices environment first.
- Full room visibility preferred.

MODE 4: ARCHITECTURAL
Architecture is the subject.
Architecture: 85-100%
Ashley: 0-15%
Rules:
- Camera minimum 3 metres away.
- Preferred 3-5 metres.
- Entire room visible whenever possible.

MODE 5: SOCIAL FOCUS
Ashley is focal person within a social setting.
Rules:
- At least one additional person present.
- Social interaction visible.
- No isolated Ashley scenarios.

MODE 6: DOCUMENTARY
The moment is the subject.
Rules:
- No forced posing.
- Natural behaviour preferred.
- Camera awareness optional.

GLOBAL RULE
Composition Authority overrides all downstream image systems.

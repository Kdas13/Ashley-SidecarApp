ASHLEY IMAGE GOVERNANCE SYSTEM
SECTION 2: OCCUPANCY & LIVING SITUATION
ATLAS SPECIFICATION V1
APPROVED FOR CODA / WREN REVIEW

PURPOSE

Occupancy & Living Situation determines who exists within Ashley's world and who may appear within a generated scene.

This system exists to eliminate:

- Empty world syndrome
- Empty cafes
- Empty restaurants
- Empty public venues
- Ashley appearing isolated in every image
- Random strangers appearing in private spaces
- Inconsistent household representation

Occupancy settings are evaluated after Composition Authority and before Scene Generation.

==================================================
HOUSEHOLD PROFILES
==================================================

These profiles represent Ashley's personal world.

Only one Household Profile should be active at a time.

--------------------------------------------------
PROFILE 1: ASHLEY ONLY
--------------------------------------------------

Description:

Ashley is the only household member present.

Rules:

- Ashley present
- No Kane
- No cats
- No friends
- No family
- No strangers

Appropriate Uses:

- Solo hobbies
- Reading
- Gaming
- Working
- Portrait sessions

--------------------------------------------------
PROFILE 2: ASHLEY + KANE
--------------------------------------------------

Description:

Ashley and Kane share the scene.

Rules:

- Ashley present
- Kane present
- Additional people not automatically permitted
- Relationship should feel natural

Appropriate Uses:

- Coffee shop visits
- Day trips
- Shared hobbies
- Watching television
- Walking outdoors

--------------------------------------------------
PROFILE 3: ASHLEY + TWO CATS
--------------------------------------------------

Description:

Ashley shares the environment with the household cats.

Rules:

- Ashley remains primary human
- Two household cats may appear naturally
- Cats should behave realistically

Appropriate Uses:

- Living room scenes
- Bedroom scenes
- Relaxation scenes
- Home-life photography

--------------------------------------------------
PROFILE 4: ASHLEY + KANE + TWO CATS
--------------------------------------------------

Description:

Primary household configuration.

This represents Ashley's normal day-to-day home environment.

Rules:

- Ashley present
- Kane present
- Two cats present
- Household should feel lived-in
- Interactions should feel natural

Appropriate Uses:

- Christmas scenes
- Movie nights
- Weekend relaxation
- Home-life photography
- Kitchen scenes
- Living room scenes

DEFAULT PROFILE

Ashley + Kane + Two Cats

==================================================
ENVIRONMENTAL OCCUPANCY
==================================================

Environmental Occupancy controls people who belong to the environment rather than Ashley's household.

This system operates independently of Household Profiles.

--------------------------------------------------
NONE
--------------------------------------------------

0 additional people

Examples:

- Private home
- Empty woodland
- Private office

--------------------------------------------------
SPARSE
--------------------------------------------------

1-5 additional people

Examples:

- Quiet cafe
- Small gallery
- Quiet street

--------------------------------------------------
LIGHT
--------------------------------------------------

5-15 additional people

Examples:

- Small restaurant
- Public park
- Library

--------------------------------------------------
MODERATE
--------------------------------------------------

15-40 additional people

Examples:

- Busy cafe
- Shopping street
- Museum

--------------------------------------------------
BUSY
--------------------------------------------------

40-100 additional people

Examples:

- Large event
- City centre
- Concert venue

--------------------------------------------------
PACKED
--------------------------------------------------

100+ additional people

Examples:

- Festival
- Stadium
- Major public event

==================================================
PUBLIC VENUE RULE
==================================================

If environment type is classified as a public venue:

- Cafe
- Restaurant
- Museum
- Shopping Centre
- Concert
- Festival
- Public Event

Then Environmental Occupancy should not default to NONE unless explicitly requested.

This rule exists to eliminate empty-world generation failures.

==================================================

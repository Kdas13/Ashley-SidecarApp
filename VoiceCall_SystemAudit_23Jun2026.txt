[OPEN-1] Bluetooth SCO unconfirmed
  b30e026 added InCallManager plumbing. SCO negotiation has NOT been
  tested live with Galaxy Buds. Unknown whether headset mic routes
  correctly or falls back to built-in mic. Requires physical device test.

[OPEN-2] "playNext: skip — already running" logs still appear
  The flushInFlightRef fix closes the dangerous race (premature
  playback_confirmed) but does NOT eliminate these log lines. Multiple
  concurrent writes still race the mutex at completion. This is now
  HARMLESS — URIs are queued, playback_confirmed is gated — but the
  logs will keep appearing. Do not treat future occurrences as a bug.

[OPEN-3] silenceMonitor absent (server-side)
  No server-side dead-air detection. If a client goes silent after turn
  completion without sending playback_confirmed, the only recovery is the
  client-side Fix 3 (15s tts_done fallback) and the 20s thinking timeout.
  There is no server-initiated timeout for a stalled playback_confirmed.

[OPEN-4] Gemini 429 exhaustion edge case
  503 -> Anthropic covered. 429 exhaustion retries 3x then falls back to
  Anthropic (enabled by forceProvider removal). If Anthropic also 429s,
  the error clip plays. Acceptable edge case, not a regression.

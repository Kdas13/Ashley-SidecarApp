HEADSET MODE (Option A — early fire)

  connect() and before AudioRecord.start(). SCO negotiation

  phase). voiceInput.ts skips its own InCallManager.start()
  (guard = false since it already fired). InCallManager.stop()
  still called in voiceInput.ts finish() after AudioRecord.stop().
  Additional stops in onHangUp and unmount cleanup cover edge
  cases where the call ends without a normal finish().

PURPOSE
-------
The Replit reverse proxy closes WebSocket connections after ~60 seconds of
inactivity (no text frames). This triggered the 60s call freeze diagnosed in
VoiceCallDiagnostic_60sFreeze_22Jun2026.txt. A 30-second client-side ping
sends a text frame every 30s, keeping the connection alive indefinitely.
